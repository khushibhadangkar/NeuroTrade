# 🧠 NeuroTrade OS

### AI-Powered Market Intelligence Platform for Indian Equities

> Institutional-grade forecasting, technical intelligence, and market analytics powered by deep learning.

NeuroTrade OS is an advanced financial intelligence platform designed to analyze, forecast, and visualize market behavior across Indian equities and indices. Combining machine learning, technical analysis, and immersive data visualization, the platform delivers actionable insights for traders, investors, researchers, and financial analysts.

Built with a custom LSTM forecasting pipeline and a modern interactive frontend, NeuroTrade transforms raw market data into intelligent predictions, probabilistic market scenarios, and real-time decision-support tools.

## 🚀 Key Highlights

* 📈 Deep Learning–based market forecasting using custom LSTM architecture
* 🎯 Probabilistic Bullish, Bearish, and Consolidation scenario generation

* 🔍 Support & resistance detection with confidence scoring

## 🧠 Core Capabilities

### AI Market Forecasting

NeuroTrade uses a stacked Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural network trained on historical market data to identify patterns, trends, and momentum shifts.

**Model Architecture**

128 → 64 → 32 → 1

Features include:

* Sequence-based time series learning
* Early stopping optimization
* Huber loss function
* Adaptive learning with Adam optimizer
* Multi-feature market input processing

---

### Market Intelligence Engine

The platform evaluates:

* Trend strength
* Momentum shifts
* Market direction
* Volatility conditions
* Institutional accumulation/distribution behavior
* Price action dynamics

Resulting insights are presented through a professional-grade intelligence dashboard.

---

### Probabilistic Scenario Analysis

Instead of providing deterministic predictions, NeuroTrade generates probability-based market outlooks.

Examples:

* Bullish Probability
* Bearish Probability
* Consolidation Probability

This approach better reflects real-world market uncertainty while supporting informed decision-making.

* Volume Analysis
* Trend Strength Indicators
* Directional Bias
* Support Zones
* Resistance Zones
* Market Structure Analysis
